Frequently Asked Questions

------------------

What types of sensors are used in GAIL's SCADA system?

We uses pressure, flow, temperature, leak detection, and valve position sensors to monitor pipeline operations.

Can the system detect leaks in real time?

Yes, advanced leak detection sensors allow for immediate identification and response to gas leaks.

What role does predictive maintenance play?

Predictive maintenance uses data analytics to anticipate potential equipment failures, reducing downtime and maintenance costs.

Is remote monitoring possible?

Yes, GAIL's SCADA system allows for centralized remote monitoring and control of gas infrastructure.

How does GAIL integrate IoT with its sensor technology?

GAIL leverages IoT devices for enhanced data connectivity and real-time insights across the gas distribution network.

What happens in case of a sensor failure?

The SCADA system includes redundancy and alerts operators to address any sensor issues promptly.
